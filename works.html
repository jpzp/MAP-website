CON - Web Series

Written by Amy Suto & Jen Enfield-Kane

Line Produced by Michael A. Perez

CON is a dramatic TV miniseries that follows two con artists: Iris, a fast-talking lone wolf, and Jonah, the idealistic architect. When their plans collide, Iris and Jonah are forced to work together to pull off their biggest cons— assuming they don’t betray each other first… CON is a story about truth told by those who lie the most.
